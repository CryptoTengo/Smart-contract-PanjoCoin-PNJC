# PNJC Liquidity Model

## Overview

The PNJC liquidity model is designed as a gradual market formation system rather than a one-time liquidity injection.

Liquidity is not fully provided at launch. Instead, it is built over time through a combination of treasury unlocks and community participation.

---

## Liquidity Structure

The liquidity system consists of two sources:

### 1. Treasury Liquidity (Internal Supply)
- 50% of total supply is locked in Liquidity Treasury
- Released over 60 months (5 years)
- Used to provide structured market liquidity over time

### 2. Community Liquidity (External Capital)
- Provided by users in USDT / POL
- Paired with PNJC tokens from treasury
- Forms decentralized exchange liquidity pools

---

## Initial Liquidity Strategy

The initial liquidity is intentionally minimal to enable organic price discovery.

Recommended starting range:

- 500 – 2,000 USDT equivalent liquidity
- Paired with PNJC tokens from Treasury allocation

This ensures:
- tradability on DEX
- controlled volatility
- organic price discovery

---

## Liquidity Growth Model

Liquidity increases through:

- treasury unlock cycles
- new user participation
- reinvestment of trading volume

No artificial liquidity inflation is used.

---

## Key Principle

> Liquidity is not pre-funded — it is progressively formed by market activity and treasury release.
