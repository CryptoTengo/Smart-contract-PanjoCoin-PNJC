# How to Provide Liquidity for PNJC

## Overview

This document explains how users can participate in the PNJC ecosystem by providing liquidity on decentralized exchanges.

---

## What is Liquidity Provision?

Liquidity providers contribute assets to a trading pool:

- PNJC tokens
- USDT or POL tokens

In return, they receive LP (Liquidity Provider) tokens representing their share of the pool.

---

## How to Participate

### Step 1: Choose a DEX
PNJC is deployed on Polygon and can be listed on:

- QuickSwap
- Uniswap (Polygon)
- Other Polygon-compatible DEXs
